All phone calls will be fairly reactive; people are usually calling with a specific transcript or case in mind and so you have to use what they say to give the information they need.

standard nice greeting aka "Hello there, how can i help you/what can i do for you"

Then pick one depending on what they say they need:

  • Not at this time.
  • Yes. There may or may not be charges associated with requesting audio on your behalf and you will be apprised of them before your transcript order goes forward.
  • Where allowed, you will receive a copy of this audio at the time of transcript completion.
  • Bankruptcy court audio does not usually incur a fee.
  • We offer an online repository that you can upload your files to once we set up a username for you. You can also mail us your media or e-mail it if the file is small enough.
  • We can also order it in some circumstances.
  • Some combination of the following can be said as appropriate:
  • We offer 28 days to pay among other terms to approved businesses.
  • In most other cases, we send you an invoice for a deposit via PayPal, no account necessary, where you can pay by credit card.
  • Generally, the deposit is 100 percent of the cost estimate of the transcript and the remaining balance is due or refunded as applicable upon completion of the transcript.
  • We accept check, money order, Zelle, and PayPal.
  • The turnaround time begins once we receive the deposit or the check clears.
  • 3, 7, 14, 30 and 45 day turnaround times as well as 24 hour turnaround on a case to case basis.
  • 10 calendar days after audio/payment are received.
  • If they want it faster than 10 calendar days, take down their name, e-mail, phone number, audio length, desired turnaround time and pass it on to me.
  • Someone will respond within 15 minutes of receiving the message.
  • Is it court or non-court?
  • For non-court, our rate is $2.00 per audio minute for a 10 calendar-day turnaround and $6.65 per page for 24-hour turnaround.
  • For court, we offer 45, 30, 14, 7, and 3 calendar-day turnarounds as well as 24-hour turnaround on a case-by-case basis.
  • electronic delivery only ($0.25 per page for a hard copy + $5.00 for a CD).

  • 30 calendar-day turnaround = $2.65 per page
  • 14 calendar-day turnaround = $3.25 per page
  • 7 calendar-day turnaround = $3.75 per page
  • 3 calendar-day turnaround = $4.25 per page
